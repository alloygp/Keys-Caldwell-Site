Current Site Cleanup — Pre-Launch Audit
Legacy URLs to redirect, noindex, or delete now that the new architecture is live
Why this matters Your current site has ~20 indexed URLs and roughly two-thirds of them are duplicates, legacy static HTML pages from 2011, orphaned WordPress drafts, or completely off-topic content. These pages split crawl budget, dilute topical authority, confuse search engines about which page is canonical, and make the site look abandoned. Cleaning this up before launching new pages is critical — Google needs to see a tight, focused site when the new content goes live, not a graveyard of duplicate services pages and evacuation zone PDFs.
